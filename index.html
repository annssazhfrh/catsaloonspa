Lion Cut

  • The term “Lion Cut” is used by cat groomers to describe the haircut done on cats when they are completely shaved down. Professional cat groomers use a set of clippers to shave the cat’s hair very short on the body
  • The most common reason for a lion cut is matting. If a cat is already matted, the best and most humane option is to shave the cat into a lion cut, then get the cat on a regular grooming schedule to prevent matting. Regular lion cuts can also be a good way to prevent a cat from becoming matted

Tips to Take Care of Cats

  • Groom your cat regularly to helps remove the dead hair from his coat so he doesn't ingest it while self-grooming
  • Provide fresh water daily because clean & fresh water is essential for your cat's good health
  • Make sure to have enough litter boxes to encourage good litter box habits, keep the litter boxes clean it will help you notice any changes in your cat's urine or stool, which could indicate a health issue
  • Train your cat to use a scratching post it will help your cat stretch her muscles and keep her claws in top condition
  • Schedule regular veterinary visits give the vet a chance to catch any potential feline diseases in their early stages, administer vaccines
